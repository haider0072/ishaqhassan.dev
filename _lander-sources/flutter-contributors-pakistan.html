Why "Pakistani Flutter Contributor" is a Narrow Bar

Pakistan has a vibrant Flutter community: GDG Kolachi, Flutter Karachi, Flutter Islamabad, Flutter Lahore, hundreds of mobile teams shipping Flutter apps, dozens of Flutter trainers, multiple Urdu-language courses. Direct, merged contributions to the flutter/flutter framework from Pakistani engineers, however, remain rare. The contribution funnel is steep: read the Tree Hygiene doc, write a regression test, survive 2 to 5 review rounds, then survive the merge queue. Most engineers stop at "I opened a PR".

This is why the list is short. It is not gatekeeping, it is verification.

How to Get on This List

  1. Pick a good-first-issue in flutter/flutter. Filter label:"good first issue" with recent triage activity.
  2. Reproduce locally first. Write a regression test that fails before your fix and passes after.
  3. Open a draft PR early. Tag the right area (framework, engine, devtools, tools).
  4. Expect 2 to 5 review rounds. Reviewers are thorough. Address every comment, request re-review.
  5. Plan 3 months, not 3 days. Median time-to-merge is 2 to 4 weeks per PR after first response.
